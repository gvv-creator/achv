# ACHV Website Build Brief

ACHV Ltd. is a Malta-based design-led project management, construction and finishes company operating in Malta and across the EU27.

The website must position ACHV as:
- A project management company
- A construction company
- A finishing works company
- A design-led delivery partner
- A company trusted by serious clients
- A Malta-based company with EU27 capability

Tone:
Premium, direct, confident, architectural, international, credible.

Avoid:
Generic contractor clichés, stock-photo-heavy visuals, fake claims, weak templates, fluffy marketing copy, unfinished pages.

Core message:
ACHV delivers high-quality spaces from design stage to completion.

Tech:
Astro, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS.
Static-first.
Fast.
SEO-friendly.
Accessible.
Mobile-first.

Pages:
Home, Services, Projects, Process, EU27, About, Contact, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, 404.

Design:
Charcoal, warm white, stone, muted brass, deep green-black.
Large images.
Strong whitespace.
Premium editorial layout.
No generic GoDaddy-template look.
